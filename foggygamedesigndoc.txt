FOGGY GAME

by sakke laitinen, jesse aaltonen, ari punavuori...


FOGGY GAME is a game with a lot of fog in it. The game stars ??? on their way through an abandoned factory to find valuable scraps, but beware as mysterious creatures will be standing in the way.


GAMEPLAY

Like a simple 2D platformer except with a limited field of vision, the fog prevents you from seeing what's coming in front of you except where there's a Limited range of a light source like torches or lanterns. The player carries a light source.


CONTROLS

[] Move
[] Jump


ITEMS

Cog: This is the basic colledtible.

Flashlight: Changes field of vision to be a cone in front of the player for a limited time.

Chainsaw: Weapon that kills monsters in front for a limited time.

Battery: Extends flashlight duration.

Fuel canister: Extends chainsaw duration.
